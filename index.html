About IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With over 400,000 members in 160+ countries, IEEE inspires a global community of innovators.

Our Student Branch

Our IEEE Student Branch at SIRCRRCOE is a vibrant community of passionate engineering students. Established in 2015, we organize workshops, hackathons, tech talks, and research symposiums, providing a platform for students to grow, innovate, and connect with industry leaders.

Our Vision

To be a premier student community that bridges the gap between academia and industry, nurturing future technology leaders who will drive meaningful innovation and make a lasting impact on society through ethical and responsible engineering.

Our Mission

To provide students with access to cutting-edge technical resources, foster collaborative learning environments, promote research and development, and create opportunities for professional growth through industry connections and global IEEE networks.
